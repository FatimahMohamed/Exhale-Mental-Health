Common Mental Health Issues

Depression

A mood disorder causing persistent sadness, loss of interest, and fatigue.

  • Symptoms: hopelessness, sleep disturbances, and difficulty concentrating.
  • Seek help if: symptoms last more than two weeks or interfere with daily life

Anxiety Disorders

Conditions characterized by excessive worry, fear, and nervousness.

  • Symptoms: restlessness, rapid heartbeat, and difficulty sleeping
  • Seek help if: anxiety disrupts daily activities or causes distress

Bipolar Disorder

A disorder causing extreme mood swings between depressive lows and manic highs.

  • Symptoms: impulsivity, racing thoughts, and periods of intense energy.
  • Seek help if: mood swings affect relationships or work

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

A condition involving unwanted repetitive thoughts and behaviors.

  • Symptoms: compulsive rituals, intrusive thoughts, and distress when routines are disrupted.
  • Seek help if: compulsions interfere with daily life

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

A disorder triggered by traumatic events, causing flashbacks, nightmares, and emotional distress.

  • Symptoms: avoidance, hypervigilance, and mood changes.
  • Seek help if: trauma-related symptoms persist

Schizophrenia

A severe mental disorder affecting thoughts, emotions, and behavior.

  • Symptoms: hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking
  • Seek help if: symptoms cause confusion or difficulty functioning

Eating Disorders

Conditions like anorexia, bulimia, and binge eating disorder that affect eating behaviors and body image.

  • Symptoms: extreme dieting, purging, or overeating
  • Seek help if: eating habits harm physical or mental health

Borderline Personality Disorder (BPD)

A disorder causing unstable emotions, relationships, and self-image.

  • Symptoms: impulsivity, fear of abandonment, and mood swings
  • Seek help if: emotional instability affects relationships

Social Anxiety Disorder

An intense fear of social situations, leading to avoidance and distress.

  • Symptoms: excessive self-consciousness, sweating, and trembling
  • Seek help if: social anxiety prevents normal interactions

Panic Disorder

A condition causing sudden, intense episodes of fear known as panic attacks.

  • Symptoms: chest pain, dizziness, and shortness of breath
  • Seek help if: panic attacks occur frequently or cause distress

Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

Chronic worry about everyday situations, often without a clear cause.

  • Symptoms: muscle tension, irritability, and difficulty concentrating.
  • Seek help if: worry feels uncontrollable

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)

A neurodevelopmental disorder affecting focus and impulse control.

  • Symptoms: difficulty paying attention, hyperactivity, and impulsiveness
  • Seek help if: Seek help if symptoms interfere with daily tasks
